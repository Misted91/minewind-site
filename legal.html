Privacy policy

This site is deliberately minimal and uses no advertising trackers and no audience analytics (no Google Analytics, no tracking cookies). The data described below is only what the features need to work.

1. Data stored in your browser

Your preferences and working data are saved locally (localStorage), on your device only. They are never sent to anyone, and you can erase them at any time by clearing the site's data in your browser.

  • minewind-theme — chosen light/dark theme.
  • minewind-lang — display language.
  • minewind-tab — last open tab.
  • minewind-builds — your saved equipment builds.
  • favorites and other interface preferences.

2. "Trade" tab (Firebase)

The Trade tab uses Firebase (a Google service) to share trade offers between visitors. This service processes:

Data Purpose
Anonymous identifier (UID), generated automatically Link your offers to a session without creating an account.
Minewind username and the content of the offers you enter Display the proposed trades to other visitors.

This data is hosted on Google Firebase infrastructure, which may be located outside the European Union. Do not enter any sensitive personal information there — use only a game username. A published offer is visible to other visitors.

3. Google Fonts

Display fonts are loaded from Google Fonts. Loading them sends your IP address to Google, solely to deliver the font files (no cookie is set in the process).

4. Retention

Local data stays until you erase it. Offers in the Trade tab remain until they are deleted (by you or by moderation).

5. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by writing to u1945074415@gmail.com. For local data, simply clear the site's data in your browser.
